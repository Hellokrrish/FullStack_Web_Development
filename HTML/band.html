Gandham Sriprasad (2 August 1979), known as Devi Sri Prasad or more commonly by his initials DSP,[2] is an Indian music composer, singer, and lyricist primarily known for his work in Telugu cinema, along with Tamil cinema.[3] He has received numerous accolades, including a National Film Award, a Nandi Award, ten Filmfare Awards, seven SIIMA Awards, and five CineMAA Awards.[4][5]
